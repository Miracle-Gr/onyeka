FAQ
What happens when I rent miners?

When you rent miners, your BNB is locked in the BNB Miner smart contract and rewards you with a daily income of 8% on the average. Sometimes your reward can be higher or lower but it's 8% on the average. Keep in mind that if you are renting miners and you already have a BNB reward, then your reward will be automatically reinvested.

Can I take my initial BNB back?

Over time! Miners need some time to mine Bucks. Every day your miners will mine the amount of bucks wich would be the 8% of your initial funds.

How much are my fees?

There is a small 4% fee in order to pay for marketing costs and developing the BNB Miner ecosystem.

When is the best time to rent miners?

Always! No matter when you rent miners, you will begin with a return rate of 8% daily.

How often should I reinvest (compound)?

We recommend that you compound at least once per day but you are free to do so as often as you wish.

How is BNB Miner sustainable?

BNB Miner is sustained by continued community support, just as every other crypto coin, token or project. The difference is that since there is no token, there is no price to dump. As long as BNB is in the contract, there will be rewards!

How do referrals work?

Once your BSC wallet is connected to the BNB Miner website, you will notice your referral address appear at the bottom of the page. When a new user buys printers after clicking your personal referral link, the contract will send a BNB value equal to 20% instantly to your wallet. When user reinvest, you also get a 20% reward. Be smart, use it to buy Printers and build yourself a stream of passive income.
